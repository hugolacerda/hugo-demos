About Gateway Business Advisors

Our Story

Gateway Business Advisors was founded on a simple belief: business owners deserve better than the typical M&A Firm experience. We combine credentialed expertise with a confidentiality-first approach that protects your business until the deal is done.

Most business brokers treat transactions as a numbers game. List as many businesses as possible, blast them out to buyers, and hope something sticks. This approach may work for the broker, but it puts sellers at risk.

We take a different approach. We are selective about the businesses we represent, we invest deeply in understanding each one, and we protect confidentiality at every step. Our clients are not just transactions. They are business owners making one of the most important financial decisions of their lives.

Based in Jacksonville, Florida, we serve business owners throughout Northeast Florida and beyond. Our team brings decades of combined experience in business sales, valuations, and exit planning.
