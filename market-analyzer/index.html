Marketing Is a Transferable Asset (or a Hidden Liability)

Most marketing tools optimize for clicks. We optimize for what buyers actually evaluate during due diligence.

Repeatability Risk

Can a new owner run your marketing system, or does it live in your head? Buyers discount businesses where acquisition is founder-dependent.

Proof Gaps

Is your acquisition explainable and evidenced? Buyers need to see documented lead sources, conversion rates, and customer acquisition costs.

Message Fragmentation

Does your brand speak with one voice? Inconsistent messaging across website, email, and ads signals operational chaos to acquirers.

Value Prop Weakness

Is your differentiation clear enough that a buyer can scale it? Vague positioning reduces multiples because it cannot be leveraged post-acquisition.
