### What They Do

Gateway Business Advisors is a Florida-based business brokerage that helps business owners
sell their companies. They serve as intermediaries in the M&A process, handling:

- Business valuation (in coordination with SBV)
- Confidential marketing to qualified buyers
- Deal negotiation and structuring
- Transaction management through closing

GBA's primary differentiator is results: Gary states an 80%+ close rate on businesses
they engage to sell. This is backed by a deliberate, selective operating model rather
than a high-volume listings approach.

**Note:** GBA does not work with first-time buyers. Gary refers all buyer inquiries out
to trusted partners. The GBA website has no buyer-facing section.

### Strategic Direction (Updated April 2026)

**GBA is being deliberately de-emphasized as Gary's primary focus.** Gary is reducing
M&A transaction work, referring out deals where the seller dynamic is difficult, and
redirecting energy toward building SBV as the primary growth vehicle. Two active deals

**Gary's 3-year exit target:** Gary intends to sell the business in approximately 3 years.
This was shared openly with the full team at the March 24 all-hands. Every infrastructure

### How GBA Operates (Key Business Philosophy)

- **Limited engagements** -- GBA does not engage more than three businesses at any given
  time. Deliberate capacity constraint, not a limitation.
- **Not a listings shop** -- The goal is to sell businesses, not list them. Gary gives
  clients honest assessments before going to market.
- **Generalist model** -- GBA works across all industries. Gary explicitly does not want
  to position GBA as an industry specialist. This is why the static industry pages were
  replaced with the Industry Insider ADS agent.
- **Team approach** -- The sales process requires a wide range of skill sets. GBA matches
  deal requirements to people who have those skills.
- **Competitive sales process** -- Every deal runs a competitive buyer process designed to
  maximize the buyer pool and optimize price, terms, and conditions.
- **Selective by design** -- If a business is not a fit for GBA, Gary refers the owner to
  a trusted partner anywhere in the United States rather than take a listing he can't close.

### ADS Agent Guiding Principle

Gary established a guiding principle for all ADS/agent development across GBA, SBV,
and any other context:

> **All conversations lead to consultation. Education leads to expertise.**

Every AI agent, regardless of topic, is a conversion tool. The agent educates the visitor.
The education builds enough trust and context that the visitor is motivated to schedule a
consultation with a human expert. This applies universally.

Gary refers to ADS agents as "The Oracle" when talking internally.

### Target Clients and Segments

GBA serves established, profitable Florida businesses typically in the $1M to $20M revenue
range. Gary segments prospects into three groups:

1. **Sell Now** -- Owner is ready to sell within 3-12 months. Primary CTA is a discovery
   consultation.
2. **Sell Later** -- Owner plans to sell in 1-5 years and wants to prepare. Focus is on
   valuation, business readiness assessment, and addressing gaps before going to market.
3. **Not Sure** -- Owner has not decided whether or when to sell. Entry point is a
   valuation and readiness assessment.

**Low-friction entry point:** GBA offers a complimentary A&C (Accounts and Conditions)
opinion of value for owners not yet ready for a full engagement.

**Priority nurture segments:** Business owners broadly, and wealth advisors specifically.
Gary considers wealth advisors the highest-priority segment for long-term email nurture.

### GBA's 5-Step Business Sales Process

**Step 1 -- Discovery:** Initial meeting, documents request, valuation/assessment, present
findings and recommendations.

**Step 2 -- Preparation:** Engagement agreement and retainer, conduct seller interview,
develop marketing materials.

**Step 3 -- Marketing:** Identify buyer prospects, initiate direct marketing, post
confidentially to business sale sites.

**Step 4 -- Buyer Management:** Pre-qualify interested parties, secure NDA, schedule
introductory calls, advise on LOI or Offer to Purchase.

**Step 5 -- Diligence and Closing:** Communicate with parties, coordinate due diligence,
review and negotiate final contract, close the deal.

## Key Terminology

- **CM&AP** -- Certified Mergers & Acquisitions Professional
- **MCBI** -- Master Certified Business Intermediary
- **CEPA** -- Certified Exit Planning Advisor
- **M&AMI** -- M&A Master Intermediary
- **CVA** -- Certified Valuation Analyst (Nancy's designation)
- **CBI** -- Certified Business Intermediary (may appear in older materials)
- **A&C** -- Accounts and Conditions opinion of value (complimentary GBA entry point)
- **SDE** -- Seller's Discretionary Earnings (common valuation metric)
- **EBITDA** -- Earnings Before Interest, Taxes, Depreciation, Amortization
- **LOI** -- Letter of Intent
- **CIM** -- Confidential Information Memorandum (shared with qualified buyers post-NDA)
- **Due Diligence** -- Buyer's investigation of the business before closing
- **Tombstone** -- A deal announcement/proof of completed transaction
- **Blind Profile** -- Business description omitting identifying details, used in early
  marketing to protect confidentiality
- **Staged Disclosure** -- GBA's process of releasing information only to qualified buyers
  at each step
- **Value Builder** -- GBA and SBV's nurture platform and assessment tool. Includes the
  Freedom Score business readiness assessment. Aaron Eiger is CIO; Kimberly is their
  consulting contact for best practices.
- **Smart Flows** -- Automated nurture sequences inside Value Builder.
- **Freedom Score** -- Business readiness assessment inside Value Builder.
- **Net Proceeds** -- What the seller walks away with after taxes, broker fees, and attorney
  fees. Example: a $5M sale might net $3M. Calculator built and live on demo.
- **The Oracle** -- Gary's informal name for ADS agents internally. Not a user-facing term.
- **EOS** -- Entrepreneurial Operating System. Management framework Gary and Nancy have
  formally adopted.
- **VTO** -- Vision/Traction Organizer. EOS's one-page strategic document.
- **L10** -- Weekly structured meeting within EOS.
- **Rocks** -- EOS term for 90-day priorities. 3-5 rocks per person per quarter.
- **Visionary / Implementer** -- EOS leadership roles. Gary is the Visionary; Nancy is
  the Implementer.
- **Profit First** -- Cash management model in use since ~2019.
- **EPI** -- Exit Planning Institute. Gary's CEPA certification body. Publishes a monthly
  list of 200-300 newly certified exit planners (mostly wealth advisors) used for targeted
  outreach.
- **Built to Sell Radio** -- Weekly Value Builder podcast. Part of the standard nurture cycle.
- **Rembrandt in the Attic** -- Gary's concept for a live webinar one week after each
  Value Builder newsletter, discussing the article in practical Main Street terms.
- **Marla Model** -- Gary's shorthand for the Raincatcher brokerage lead generation
  approach: high-volume, low-cost digital marketing driving prospects to a business
  readiness survey, converting a percentage to paid services. Gary wants to replicate
  this for SBV. See KB_08_SBV_STRATEGY.md.
- **NGF (Net Growth Factor)** -- Flint's metric for evaluating campaign ROI. NGF = Average
  Customer Value minus Customer Acquisition Cost. Any positive NGF means you invest more
  in the channel. Gary maps this to LTV:CAC ratios he uses in software valuations.
- **ICP** -- Ideal Customer Profile. First step in Flint's product-building framework.
  Use ICP Builder or Marketing Genius in MeclabsAI.
- **CFS (Customer First Solution)** -- Formerly called CFO (Customer First Objective),
  renamed by Flint to avoid confusion with Chief Financial Officer. Identifies the problem
  to solve (to), the solution (by), and what you ask in exchange (for).
- **SCA** -- Summary Competitive Analysis. Third step in Flint's framework. Marketing
  Genius can run it.
- **PVP** -- Product Value Prop. Fourth step in Flint's framework. Can't be written
  until ICP, CFS, and SCA are complete.
- **GHL** -- GoHighLevel. CRM and marketing automation platform being adopted to replace
  Pipeline CRM. See KB_10_GHL_CRM.md for full details.
