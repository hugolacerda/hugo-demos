8 Recurring Revenue Ideas for Any Business Owner

March 4, 2025
← Back to Insights

Recurring revenue is a smart way to build consistent income and strengthen customer relationships. Here are eight simple ways any business can add recurring revenue to the mix:

Offer customers a monthly or yearly subscription for regular access to products or services. Works great for digital and physical goods alike.

Example: A coffee shop offering a monthly coffee plan with a set number of drinks.

Set up a membership for exclusive perks or benefits. Perfect for businesses with loyal customers who want extra value.

Example: A gym with tiered memberships offering different class and equipment access.

Provide ongoing services, like maintenance or support, for a monthly fee. This model works well for tech, repair, or consulting services.

Example: An IT firm offering monthly support contracts for regular tech maintenance.

Have equipment or tools? Rent or lease them out! This is ideal for products people only need temporarily.

Example: A photography shop renting cameras and lighting on a monthly or weekly basis.

For consumable products, offer a refill program to keep customers stocked up. Ideal for items that need regular replacement.

Example: A pet food company offering monthly deliveries based on customer needs.

If you have valuable intellectual property, license it! This works well for software, media, and creative products.

Example: A software company charging monthly fees for businesses to access their platform.

Offer a monthly retainer so clients get priority access to your services. Perfect for consultants, lawyers, and freelancers.

Example: A marketing consultant offering a retainer with a set number of hours per month.

Sell peace of mind by offering warranties or insurance plans. Great for products that might need replacement or repair.

Example: An appliance store offering yearly warranty plans on major purchases.

In Short: Recurring revenue models, like subscriptions, memberships, and retainers, can add reliable income and boost customer loyalty. Try out one that fits your business to keep income steady and customers happy!
